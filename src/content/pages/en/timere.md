# Timers

Here you will find a number of timers that have been used in Norwegian tournaments and local leagues. A table hockey match lasts 5 minutes. 30 seconds of music are played before the start signal and 30 seconds of music before the end signal. At Norwegian tournaments, there are also signals when 1/3 and 2/3 of the match has passed.
