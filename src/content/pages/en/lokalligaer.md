## The Jæren-league

<img src="/media/images/lokalligaer-a5c66f5e.jpg" alt="Play in the Jæren league" class="league-photo" loading="lazy">

The Jæren-league is played every Wednesday at 6pm in the Kvernhallen at Kvernaland.

Join [the Spond group](https://spond.com/landing/group/MQVXO) to get invitations to leagues. Contact person: Andreas Fjermestad
