## Local leagues

## Do you live in Bergen or the Stavanger area?

We have active local leagues that play table hockey weekly in both Bergen and Kvernaland. Players of all ages and skill levels are welcome to participate!

[Join a local league](/en/lokalligaer)

## Learn table hockey

## Do you want to get better at table hockey?

Whether you're a beginner or experienced, we offer resources to help you improve your techniques, understand the tactics of the game, and dominate the boards!

## Tournaments

## Are you ready to take the next step?

5 official NBHF tournaments are organized each year in Bergen, Trondheim and Kvernaland. The tournaments are open to everyone, and are free for players in their first season!

[Sign up for a tournament](/en)
