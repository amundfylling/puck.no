For the first time in history, the Duo Norwegian Championship will be held. In Duo-NM, two players play on each team.

General rules

\- Team size: Each team consists of exactly two participants. Substitutes are only allowed for mixed teams.

\- Club affiliation: The players on a team do not have to belong to the same club.

Group stage:

The group stage is played over two rounds.

The result is decided by summing the goals scored in both games. The team with the most goals in total wins.

Playoffs:

The playoffs are decided in a best-of-7 format.

The winner of each round in the playoffs is found by summing the goals from both games. The team with the most goals in total wins the round.
