The Norwegian Table Hockey Association is once again inviting the table hockey community to Norway for the annual World Tour tournament, Norway Open. This year's tournament will be hosted in Kvernaland, which is the largest table hockey community in Norway.

Transportation on game day will be provided from Sandnes or Bryne to ensure a smooth transition for all participants. For your accommodation needs, we recommend staying in either Sandnes or Bryne. Sandnes is easy to get to from the airport, taking the 42 bus which goes directly to Sandnes City Centre. If public transport is not easily accessible to your hotel, we will do our best to help with shuttle.

Playing system:

Group stage with two groups. Top 8 from each group will go through to final groups. The rest will play in division 1.

Note: points taken in the group stage will count in both final group and division 1.

1/8 play off in final groups, best of 7.

Quarterfinals play off in division 1, best of 5.

Prices:

Senior players: 300 NOK

Foreigner: 200 NOK

Junior: 150 NOK

Under 13: Free
