The 2025 Table Hockey World Championships were held in Umeå, Sweden, and became an unforgettable weekend for the Norwegian national team. A huge thank you to the organizers for a highly professional and memorable championship!

## Historic medal in the women's category

![Norway's women's team. From the left: Ane Skadsem, Kristin Fjermestad, Anne Grethe Gangsøy, Linnea Gangsøy and Mona Gangsøy](/media/images/norge-med-gull-og-bronse-i-vm-i-umeå-ee3db520.jpeg)

Norway's women's team. From the left: Ane Skadsem, Kristin Fjermestad, Anne Grethe Gangsøy, Linnea Gangsøy and Mona Gangsøy

The championship kicked off with a bang as the Norwegian women's team took bronze in the team competition – Norway's **first ever medal in the women's category**. Congratulations to Ane Skadsem, Kristin Fjermestad, Anne Grethe Gangsøy, Linnea Gangsøy and Mona Gangsøy for a brilliant performance and a perfect start to the weekend!

## Gold in the super veterans category

![Yngve on the podium together with Truls Månsson (left) and Igor Saveljev (right)](/media/images/norge-med-gull-og-bronse-i-vm-i-umeå-26be8d20.jpeg)

Yngve on the podium together with Truls Månsson (left) and Igor Saveljev (right)

Yngve Aasheim claimed **Norway's only gold** in the super veterans category, repeating his triumph from 2019.
