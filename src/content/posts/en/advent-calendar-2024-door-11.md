Behind door 10 we found the table hockey legend Tore Lie. At his best he was ranked no. 7 in the world, and with his 34 gold medals in NBHF tournaments he still leads Magnus Klippen in the medal count. We move on to door 11.

**1\. What is/was your favourite combination or trick?**

The Jon spesial. Especially if I manage to lure the defenceman all the way up into the slot and score from a tight angle.

**2\. What is your favourite lunch at a table hockey tournament?**

A packed Byggrynslunsj from Fjordland and chocolate milk. Anything to avoid the farce (Not really).

**3\. Which tournament do/did you like travelling to the most?**

The Norwegian Championship. The Championship is a treat, with a Gangsøy quiz in the car on the way there (I get 0 points due to having no general knowledge).

**4\. What is your best table hockey moment?**

My first win in a best-of and a medal in the B division at the Norwegian Championship. Silver or bronze I think,.. could also have been gold? God knows, can't be bothered going down to the cellar to check the trophy. A great and intense moment it was, in any case.

**5\. When do you get up in the morning if the tournament starts at 10:00, and you have a 15-minute trip to the venue?**

09:30.
