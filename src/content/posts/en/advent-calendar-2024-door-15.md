Behind door 14 we found Andreas Fjermestad. The overall top 10 therefore looks like this after 14 doors:

![](/media/images/adventskalender-2024-luke-15-fe1e6f26.png)

Who is hiding behind today's door?

**1\. What is/was your favourite combination or trick?**

Boring to say, but I have to go with Spjass because that was the one that decided the results.

**2\. What is your favourite lunch at a table hockey tournament?**

Jim's homemade baguettes. They were at a level you haven't seen before or since.

**3\. Which tournament do/did you like travelling to the most?**

Kvernaland Open.

**4\. What is your best table hockey moment?**

Knocking out Horvath in a best of 7 in a clash that had plenty of verbal and psychological shenanigans in it. Horvath wanted to shake hands after every single game. I wanted to shake hands only after the full 7-game series. You say thanks for the meal. Not for the potatoes, carrots and sauce. All the greatest experiences are tied to best of seven. That's where you get many ups and downs in a short space of time 😊

**5\. When do you get up in the morning if the tournament starts at 10:00, and you have a 15-minute trip to the venue?**

Early. 7 maybe. - either because I'm helping organise it, or because I like to be out very early. Drinking coffee in the back room is half the tournament 😊
