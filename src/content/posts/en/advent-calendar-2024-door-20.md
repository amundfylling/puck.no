Pål Bakken was hiding behind yesterday's door. Christmas Eve is approaching at breakneck speed, but luckily we still have some treats left. Who do we find behind door 20?

**1\. What is/was your favourite combination or trick?**

I can't quite remember what it's called, but it's from the left defenceman to the winger. It's on the opposite side of what Frode usually does, so maybe we could call it a "reverse bulldozer"? Works once in ten tries. The main goal is to spread fear.

**2\. What is your favourite lunch at a table hockey tournament?**

Nothing beats a hot meal followed by something sweet. A lot of people make the mistake of underestimating the latter.

**3\. Which tournament did/do you most enjoy travelling to?**

Jæren is clearly the favourite. There's something about the special table hockey culture there – nice people, a great atmosphere, and hosts who always go that extra mile.

**4\. What is your best table hockey moment?**

That time Hans Østerman asked whether Jørn Moldvær also knew that "fantastic shot" I used against Østerman.

**5\. When do you get up in the morning if the tournament starts at 10:00, and it's a 15-minute trip to the venue?**

Seven thirty. I like to have plenty of time for breakfast and, most importantly, to make sure everything else is out of the system before the tournament starts. Nobody wants to take a dump mid-tournament.
