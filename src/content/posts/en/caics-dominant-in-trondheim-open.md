Edgars Caics unsurprisingly took the win at Trondheim Open 2025. The reigning European champion thus claimed his first individual victory on Norwegian soil at his sixth attempt. In the final he faced Yngve Aasheim, who pulled himself together for the playoffs after a somewhat slow start in the group stage. Amund Risa Fylling and Gustav Brådland shared third place. You can find the complete results from the tournament [here](https://th.sportscorpion.com/eng/tournament/id/7050/).
