Finally we got some variation in the answers, and the correct answer was a Trønder living in Eastern Norway: Hallvard Einer Sægrov. Which table hockey player is hiding behind door 5?

**1\. What is/was your favourite combination or trick?**

Standing still shot from the left wing into the nearest cross, especially on boards with a livestream and camera on the left side of the board. Euphoric.

**2\. What is your favourite lunch at a table hockey tournament?**

Any food offered during the tournament, so you can skip the cold trip to the shop, is received with gratitude. We're also trying to phase down the consumption of energy drinks during tournaments, so water would have to be a favourite.

**3\. Which tournament do/did you like travelling to the most?**

There's something special about representing your country together with your countrymen. The World Championships.

**4\. What is your best table hockey moment?**

Seeing little brother Klippen running around the playing hall while screaming and kicking pucks, after winning the semi-final against middle brother in the Bergen Open.

**5\. When do you get up in the morning if the tournament starts at 10:00, and you have a 15-minute trip to the playing hall?**

09:00.
