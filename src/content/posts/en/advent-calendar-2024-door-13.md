Behind door 12 was the eternal youngster Yngve Aasheim. Before Magnus Klippen's total dominance of Norwegian table hockey, Yngve won no fewer than four Norwegian championships in a row (2013-2016). He also made it all the way to the quarter-finals of the 2015 World Championship in Moscow, where he knocked out the Russian top players Alexey Zaharov and Alexey Titov - definitely one of the greatest Norwegian table hockey moments in history!

Many got yesterday's door right. How many will crack today's door?

**1\. What is/was your favourite combination or trick?**

Kiosk!

**2\. What is your favourite lunch at a table hockey tournament?**

Jim's chicken soup

**3\. Which tournament did/do you most enjoy travelling to?**

The tournament at Jæren, with an overnight stay at Svein Hestvaag's place.

**4\. What is your best table hockey moment?**

Many great moments - but my best result was probably in the Øresund Cup.

**5\. When do you get up in the morning if the tournament starts at 10:00, and you have a 15-minute journey to the venue?**

Being the gentleman I am, I get up well in advance, so that both personal hygiene and a proper meal in the company of good friends can be taken care of before the tournament.
