We headed back to Jæren, and the correct answer was Juvelvegen's finest player: Daniel Nygård. Which table hockey player is hiding behind door 6?

**1\. What is/was your favourite combination or trick?**

Right shovel on the underside of the opponent's left defenceman.

**2\. What is your favourite lunch at a table hockey tournament?**

I like it when there's an organised lunch at the venue. Pizza, the grub on offer, or burgers all work fine.

**3\. Which tournament do/did you enjoy travelling to the most?**

Lots of great tournaments at Jæren. Still, there's something really special about Trondheim Open, since they always use the same venue — and that's where I played my first NBA tournament.

**4\. What is your best table hockey moment?**

Lag-NM 2010.

**5\. When do you get up in the morning if the tournament starts at 10:00, and you have a 15-minute trip to the venue?**

A few years ago it could easily have been 09:30, but these days it's as a rule no later than 08:00.
